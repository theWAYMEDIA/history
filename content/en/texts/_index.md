title: "Primary Texts"
description: "Sacred texts, foundational scriptures, and primary documents of the Wayist tradition — source materials that have carried the teaching across millennia."
weight: 35
---

The primary texts of the Wayist tradition are distinguished from historical analysis about the tradition: these are the documents themselves. Some are ancient in origin; some were composed in specific historical moments; all have functioned as carriers of the living teaching across time and culture.

Where analytical articles interpret and contextualise, primary texts speak directly. The reader encounters the teaching as those who received it first encountered it — without the mediating layer of scholarly commentary.
